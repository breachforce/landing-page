Frequently Asked Questions

BreachForce is Mumbai's underground cybersecurity meetup community. We're a free, grassroots community of hackers, red teamers, defenders, and curious minds who gather monthly to share knowledge, discuss the latest security research, and learn from each other.

Our focus is on practical, hands-on cybersecurity content without vendor pitches or sales presentations. We believe in community-driven learning and providing a platform for real-world security discussions.

Our meetups are open to everyone interested in cybersecurity - from complete beginners to seasoned professionals. Whether you're a student, security researcher, penetration tester, bug bounty hunter, or just curious about cybersecurity, you're welcome to join our community.

All BreachForce meetups are completely free to attend! We're a non-profit, community-driven initiative focused on knowledge sharing and learning. Our goal is to make cybersecurity education accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

We're always looking for speakers to share their knowledge and research! You can submit your talk proposal through our CFP (Call for Papers) portal. We welcome talks on all cybersecurity topics including penetration testing, vulnerability research, malware analysis, defensive techniques, and more. Both experienced speakers and first-time presenters are encouraged to apply.
